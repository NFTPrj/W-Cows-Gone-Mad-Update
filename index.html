The Lore


A long time ago, planet Earth was ruled by Humans. Humans farmed other animals for food and resources, but it was one action that nearly led to their demise…


Humans began to feed cattle their deceased relatives.


An infectious brain disease came in to existence which would spread across planet Earth….


A collection of cows survived the outbreak. They are affected but not terminal.


The condition means they will forever have certain behavioural issues but has also resulted in the development of unique abilities.


The Cows have undergone a mutation, giving them superior intelligence and now they are about to enter the Metaverse.





Collection


Cows Gone Mad (CGM) is a collection of 3,500 unique but equally mad cows living on the Solana blockchain.


These were the only cows fortunate enough to survive the fallout of the mad cow disease outbreak in the 80s and 90s and have re-emerged to cause havoc as they attempt to enter into the Metaverse.


About CGM


Cows Gone Mad (CGM) is an NFT project and is the brainchild of Dr Ricardo Persaud BTEC HNC, MB BS (Lond), MPHIL, MSB, CBIOL, DOHNS, MRCS (Gen Surg), FRCS (ORL-HNS), OCT (Otol) (UK), Consultant ENT Surgeon and Head of Department, NMC Royal Hospital Sharjah, UAE


Dr Ricardo was part of the global medical task force assigned to the mission of fighting the mad cow disease outbreak. The disease affected cattle across the globe during the 80s and 90s. This moment in history had far reaching implications with millions of cattle being culled in order to rid the world of Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy (BSE), commonly known as mad cow disease.

The artwork for this project was inspired by his experience. the project itself aims to educate people of the potential dangers when humans interfere with nature.

The project will consist of a number of phases, with the first phase being the release of 3,500 unique NFTs on the Solana blockchain.
